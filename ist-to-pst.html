IST (Indian Standard Time) is UTC+5:30. India does not observe Daylight Saving Time.

PST (Pacific Standard Time) is UTC-8:00 (during standard time) or UTC-7:00 (during daylight saving time as PDT). The time difference between IST and PST is typically 13 hours and 30 minutes, with IST being ahead.
